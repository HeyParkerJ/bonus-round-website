The Only Nerd Hub In Phoenix

Geek Out & Game On with some of the best craft cocktails in the valley, a killer rotating draft menu, delicious food, and 15 of your favorite classic arcade games always set to FREE PLAY! Whether you are meeting up with friends, playing tabletop games, chilling at the bar, or just reading your favorite comic book, this is the place to be. Bonus Round is for everyone - the Nerds, Geeks, Hipsters, Gamers, and Underdogs - a place to come and be yourself, celebrate your passions, embrace your Nerdhood.
